Iron's Spells & Spellbooks

Iron's Spells & Spellbooks is a Minecraft magic mod inspired by classic RPG spellcasting. Its goal is to bring a deep, progressive spellcasting system into Minecraft, where players learn spells, craft magical gear, explore new structures, and fight magical foes.

Core Features

  • Over 100 upgradable spells covering multiple schools of magic (Fire, Ice, Blood, Holy, Evocation, Ender, Eldritch, etc.).
  • New boss fights and magical enemies: 2 spellcasting bosses, as well as merchants, allies, and hostile spellcasters.
  • Magical armor sets and gear: multiple wizard armor sets designed to enhance mana, cool-down reduction, and spell power.
  • Randomly generated magical structures to explore, filled with loot, scrolls, and challenges.
  • Weapon imbuement and armor upgrade systems to enhance and bind spells to gear.
  • Compatibility with popular mods (JEI, JEED, Apotheosis, Tetra, Better Combat, Curios, Patchouli) and configurable options per spell.
